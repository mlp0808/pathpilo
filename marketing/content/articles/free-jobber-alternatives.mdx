If you've searched "free Jobber alternative" recently, you've probably found articles that list Housecall Pro, ZenMaid, and FieldPulse as free options. They're not. They're 14-day trials. Listing them as "free" is either laziness or intentional misdirection, and you'll only discover the truth when the trial ends and the credit card request arrives.

**[Jobber](https://getjobber.com/pricing/) itself has no free plan.** The lowest Jobber tier starts at $28/month (billed monthly) after a 14-day trial. That is a reasonable product at a fair price — but it is not free, and you deserve to know that upfront before comparing alternatives.

This article contains only the 7 field service and scheduling tools that have a genuine free tier as of July 2026 — with real numbers, real limits, and an honest assessment of who each one is actually useful for.

## When the free plan isn't enough: how to think about upgrading

For most field service businesses, the free-to-paid trigger isn't a feature you need urgently — it's a scale threshold you grow into:

- **PathPilo:** stays free until you're running two technicians on separate routes simultaneously on the same day. A sole trader or a business where only one person is in the field stays free indefinitely.
- **ServiceM8:** hits the cap when you exceed 30 active jobs a month — which happens within the first week for most active operators.
- **Workiz:** hits the cap at 20 jobs in a month — essentially day 4 for any active business.

This matters because it tells you which free plans are actually designed to run your business long-term, and which ones are designed to convert you quickly. PathPilo is built to stay free for solo and single-route operations indefinitely. Most others are designed to hit a limit within 2 to 4 weeks.

## What Jobber offers that most free alternatives don't

- You want to validate your workflow before committing to $28–$245/month

## The right question to ask

Before choosing between any of these tools, ask yourself: **what specifically is not working right now?**

- If the answer is "I spend too long planning routes" → you need route planning (PathPilo free covers this; most others don't at the free tier)
- If the answer is "I lose track of which jobs are booked" → you need job management (PathPilo, ServiceM8 within the cap)
- If the answer is "I'm not getting paid fast enough" → you need invoicing automation (PathPilo, ServiceM8, Workiz within caps)
- If the answer is "I can't manage my team's shifts and hours" → you need workforce management (Connecteam, Homebase)

Matching the tool to the actual problem saves you three weeks of evaluating the wrong platform.

## Frequently asked questions

### Does Jobber have a free plan?

No. Jobber offers a 14-day free trial, after which pricing starts at $28/month (billed monthly). There is no permanent free tier.

### What is the best free alternative to Jobber for cleaning businesses?

PathPilo is the most capable free option for cleaning businesses — unlimited clients, recurring job scheduling, route planning, and invoicing are all included on the free plan. For a cleaning company with one active crew running at a time, PathPilo is free indefinitely. ZenMaid, which was previously on free-alternative lists with a "Forever Free" plan, appears to have moved to a paid-only model as of July 2026 — verify their current plans at [zenmaid.com/pricing](https://www.zenmaid.com/pricing/).

### What is the best free alternative to Jobber for lawn care?

PathPilo handles the core lawn care workflow free: recurring client scheduling, route optimisation, job tracking, and invoicing. GorillaDesk is a strong paid option for lawn care and pest control but starts at $49/month. No specifically free GorillaDesk tier exists.

### Is ServiceM8 really free?

Yes, genuinely — but with a hard cap of 30 active jobs per month per user. This is a real free plan, not a trial, but it limits most active service businesses within the first week. It is most suitable for very early-stage solo operators or as an extended evaluation of the platform.

### What does PathPilo's free plan actually include?

Unlimited jobs, unlimited clients, full scheduling, route planning with map view, invoicing and quotes, client management, job history, and a mobile app for field technicians. The free plan is designed to run a complete solo or single-route service operation — not just test the software. A paid plan is needed when you're running multiple employees on separate routes in the same day.

### Are there any free field service apps with route planning?

PathPilo is the only tool on this list that includes genuine route planning on the free tier. Most free plans from FSM tools do not include route optimisation — it's typically a paid feature. This is the most significant differentiator between PathPilo's free plan and every other option on this list.

### What's the difference between a "scheduling tool," a "team management tool," and a "full field service platform"?

A **scheduling tool** (Setmore, Square Appointments) manages appointments and calendars. A **team management tool** (Connecteam, Homebase) manages employee shifts, time tracking, and internal communication. A **full field service platform** (PathPilo, Jobber, ServiceM8) manages the entire workflow: clients → scheduling → routing → mobile execution → invoicing → payment. When searching for a Jobber alternative, you typically want the third category.
